Our story

Built by the tide, for thirty years

From a single beach cottage in 1996 to a full coastal retreat — Baharini Bay has always belonged to the ocean first.

How it started

A family cottage, a coral reef, and a promise

Baharini Bay began as three rooms behind a fisherman's kitchen. Guests came for the reef and stayed for the quiet — no loud resorts, no rush, just the tide clock deciding the day.

Today we're still family-run. Every member of staff, from the dhow captain to the head chef, grew up along this stretch of coast, and it shows in how the place is looked after.

What we hold onto

Three values, unchanged since 1996

Reef-first

No jet skis over the reef, reef-safe sunscreen only, and a marine guide on every snorkel trip.

Locally run

Over 90% of our team is from villages within 10km of the resort.

Coast on a plate

Our kitchen buys straight from the morning boats — nothing frozen, nothing flown in.
